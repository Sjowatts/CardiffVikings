Cardiff Vikings Join SWBA East Division 3!

We’re proud to announce that Cardiff Vikings Basketball Club has officially been registered to compete in the SWBA East Division 3 for the 2025/26 season! 🏀

This marks a huge step forward for the club, as we move from friendlies and foundation-building into full league competition. The journey so far has been built on commitment, community, and a shared passion for the game — and this next chapter promises even more.

We’re looking forward to testing ourselves against some of the toughest grassroots talent in South Wales and continuing to grow both on and off the court.

📅 Fixtures will be released later this summer.
📲 Stay tuned for player signings, preseason updates, and ways to get involved.

Let’s go, Vikings! ⚔️
