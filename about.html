About Cardiff Vikings

Cardiff Vikings is a passionate, player-driven basketball club founded by a group of former university teammates committed to continuing their journey in the sport. Built on ambition, unity, and a love for the game, the team has quickly grown into a close-knit squad aiming to compete at higher levels in Welsh basketball and beyond.

The club is led by Sam Watts, who serves as owner, head coach, and player. With a master’s in performance analysis and years of coaching experience starting from the age of 12, Sam’s vision is to create a performance-first environment where players can grow technically, tactically, and personally.

Our goal is to become one of the top amateur clubs in Wales, progressing through league systems with the long-term aim of reaching the British Basketball League. We also plan to expand into women’s and junior teams, ensuring basketball is accessible for all and deeply rooted in the local community.

At Cardiff Vikings, we’re not just building a team — we’re building a basketball culture.
